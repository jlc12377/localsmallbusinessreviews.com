About Miami's Concierge Medicine Sector

Why Miami concierge medicine practices matter and what to look for.

Concierge medicine has transformed Miami's healthcare landscape, particularly in Miami Beach, Coral Gables, and Brickell. Patients increasingly seek the personalized access, longer appointment times, and 24/7 availability that traditional primary care can no longer provide.

The right concierge practice understands Miami's diverse patient base — multilingual communication, cultural considerations, international patient services, and the spectrum of insurance and self-pay arrangements. Some practices specialize in internal medicine, others in pediatrics, others in functional or preventive medicine.

Our rankings prioritize practices with verified credentials, established Miami presence, hospital affiliations where relevant, and clear service models — operators delivering demonstrated quality of care to Miami patients.

Our Ranking Methodology

Public Ratings (35%)

Aggregated ratings from Yelp, Google Business, and patient review platforms.

Hospital Affiliations & Credentials (25%)

Board certifications, hospital affiliations, and verified medical credentials.

Industry Recognition (20%)

Recognition from Modern Luxury, U.S. News, and industry publications covering Miami concierge medicine.

Service Model Clarity (20%)

Clear concierge structure, accessibility commitments, and patient panel size discipline.
