About Miami's Real Estate Market

Why Miami real estate firms matter and what to look for.

Miami's real estate market spans some of the most distinct submarkets in Florida. Brickell and Downtown Miami are dominated by luxury high-rise condos. Coral Gables, Coconut Grove, and Pinecrest serve the established residential market. Wynwood, Edgewater, and Midtown attract investors. Miami Beach, Surfside, and Bal Harbour remain ultra-luxury markets.

The right real estate firm understands these submarkets at a granular level — pricing trends, building reputations, neighborhood dynamics, and the practical realities of Miami's competitive environment.

Our rankings prioritize firms with verified transaction history, established Miami presence, and demonstrated client service standards.

Our Ranking Methodology

Transaction Volume (35%)

Verified annual sales volume and career sales totals from RealTrends, U.S. News, and firm-reported data.

Public Ratings (25%)

Aggregated ratings from Google Business, Yelp, and Better Business Bureau where applicable.

Industry Recognition (20%)

Awards and rankings from RealTrends, Florida Realtors, U.S. News, and industry publications.

Local Specialization (20%)

Demonstrated expertise in specific Miami submarkets, property types, or client segments.
